About Online Keyboards

Free virtual keyboards for typing in Urdu, Sindhi, and Arabic — right in your browser.

What Is Online Keyboards?

Online Keyboards is a free web-based tool that lets you type in Urdu, Sindhi, and Arabic without installing any software or changing your system keyboard settings. Whether you're writing an email, composing a social media post, or working on a document, our keyboards make it easy to type in your preferred language.

How It Works

Simply visit the keyboard page for your language, and start typing. You can either:

  • Click on the virtual keys using your mouse or touchscreen
  • Use your physical keyboard — each key maps to the corresponding character shown on the virtual keyboard

Once you've typed your text, you can copy it to your clipboard, save it as a file, print it, or share it via email, WhatsApp, or social media.

Features

  • 🇵🇰 Urdu Keyboard — Standard and Phonetic layouts
  • 🕌 Sindhi Keyboard — Full Arabic-script Sindhi layout
  • 🇸🇦 Arabic Keyboard — Standard and AZERTY layouts
  • 📋 Copy, save, and print your typed text
  • 🔊 Text-to-Speech — hear your text read aloud
  • 💬 Share via WhatsApp, Twitter, Email, and Google Translate
  • 🌙 Dark mode for comfortable typing at night
  • 📱 Fully responsive — works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • ⚡ Fast loading — no heavy libraries or animations

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Esc — Toggle between the language keyboard and QWERTY passthrough mode
  • Shift — Access shifted characters (shown in blue on the keys)
  • Caps Lock — Toggle uppercase for phonetic keyboards
  • Ctrl+C — Copy selected text
  • Ctrl+A — Select all text

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to install anything?

No! Online Keyboards works entirely in your browser. No downloads, no plugins, no extensions needed.

Can I use my physical keyboard?

Yes. When the keyboard mode is active, pressing keys on your physical keyboard will type the corresponding Urdu/Sindhi/Arabic character. Press Esc to switch back to normal QWERTY mode.

Is it free to use?

Absolutely. Online Keyboards is completely free with no registration required.

Does it work on mobile phones?

Yes. The keyboards are fully responsive and work on any device with a web browser.

What is the difference between Standard and Phonetic?

The Standard layout maps characters based on the official keyboard layout used in that language's region. The Phonetic layout maps characters to their closest-sounding English letter (e.g., pressing 'A' types 'ا').
